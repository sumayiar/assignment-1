Cheetah

Cheetahs are a golden-yellow coat with black spots painted over their body. This helps them camouflage in their grassland habitats. Their native to sub-Saharan Africa and roam in savannahs, grasslands, scrublands, and semi-arid regions. As carnivores, they hunt medium-sized ungulates such as gazelles and impalas. However, they prefer to stay on the ground since they're not the greatest climbers like most cats! Instead, they stalk and chase their prey over short distances. Unfortunately, they're listed as "Vulnerable" on the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) Red List of Threatened Species. They face threats such as habitat loss and humans attempting to poach their skins. Help conserve cheetahs and their habitats here.
