Monkey Business Hub

Swinging through sun-dappled canopies, monkeys reign supreme in tropical forests. They are known to be one of the most diverse groups of mammals and about 260 species are discovered as of today. Monkeys are found in various habitats including rainforests, savannas, and mountainous regions. These animals typically live for up to 15 to 25 years. Monkeys are also one of the species that almost act like humans are very intelligence creatures having the ability to problem solve.
