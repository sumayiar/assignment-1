Flamingo Fantasia

Flamingoes, the graceful titans of the shallows, strut through tropical wetlands like living bouquets. Standing on one leg like seasoned ballerinas, these long-limbed beauties filter brine shrimp and algae with their elegant, curved beaks, painting the water with a mesmerizing pink hue. These creatures live up to 20 to 30 years. These birds are highly social and often live in larger colonies that can be up to thousands of individuals. Even though these animals are very large they have very strong fliers and are very strong they can cover long distances while migrating and their wings are beautiful colors when they extend.
